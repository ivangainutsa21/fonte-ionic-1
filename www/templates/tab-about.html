Fountain of Life is a joint partnership between Equip Mozambique, international partners, and the local african churches. While Fountain of Life was originally created for Mozambique, it is now available in all Portuguese-speaking countries in Africa and is coming to all of the English-speaking countries on the continent by the end of 2017.

The goal of the application is to provide resources to the local believers in a language and method they can understand. Any church is able to upload resources on the app provided they follow the guidelines.

If you want to upload your resources to benefit fellow believers of your language, please click the link above or E-mail us for more information. We are always looking for more organizations to partner with and would love to hear from you.

If you believe there is a copyright infringement of any of the material provided, please fill out form located here.

Fonte da Vida é uma parceria conjunta entre Equip Mozambique, organizaçoes internacionais, e a igreja local em Africa.
